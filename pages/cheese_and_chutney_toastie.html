Ingredients

  • White or brown bread
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Chutney
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Pre head toastie maker
  2. Butter bread
  3. Cut slices of cheddar cheese and place on buttered peice of bread
  4. Use a spoon to spread some chutney on the cheese
  5. Place toastie in the toastie maker and cook until the cheese has melted and the bread has started to go golden brown
