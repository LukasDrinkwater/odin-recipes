Ingredients

  • White or brown bread
  • Cheddar cheese. Or a cheese of your choice
  • Butter
  • Smoked bacon
  • Blue cheese for extra tang

Steps

  1. Start grilling the bacon
  2. Pre head the toastie maker
  3. Butter two slices of bread
  4. Cut some slices of cheddar cheese and however much blue cheese you want
  5. Place the cheese on one peice of bread
  6. When the bacon is cooked to your liking plcae it ontop of the cheese and place the toastie in the toastie maker.
  7. When the cheese has meleted and the bread has started to go golden place the toastie on a plate and cut into two big triangles
